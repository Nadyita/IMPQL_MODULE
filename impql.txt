<header2>Usage:<end>

Show stats for implants at a given QL:
<tab><highlight><symbol>impql implant_ql<end>

Find the highest QL of an implant you can equip with given ability and treatment:
<tab><highlight><symbol>impql attribute_value treatment_value<end>

<header2>Examples:<end>

<highlight><symbol>impql 150<end>
<tab><cyan>[Bot]: <end>QL <highlight>150<end> <a href="">Implant details</a> and <a href="">Jobe Implant details</a>.

<highlight><symbol>impql 200 500<end>
<tab><cyan>[Bot]: <end>With <highlight>200<end> Ability and <highlight>500<end> Treatment, the highest possible <a href="">Implant</a> is QL <highlight>98<end>.

<highlight><symbol>impql 300 720<end>
<tab><cyan>[Bot]: <end>With <highlight>300<end> Ability and <highlight>720<end> Treatment, the highest possible <a href="">Implant</a> is QL <highlight>148<end> and the highest possible <a href="">Jobe Implant</a> is QL <highlight>143<end>.

<header2>Notice:<end>

<tab>Ability Clusters:
<tab><tab><black>0<end><highlight>42<end> (QL <highlight>147<end> - QL <highlight>150<end>) Shiny <header>-><end> <highlight>306<end> / <highlight>720<end>

This means that the shiny cluster will give you <highlight>+42<end> in any ability from QL <highlight>147<end> to QL <highlight>150<end>.
To reach the next highest bonus (<highlight>+43<end> at QL <highlight>151<end>), you will need at least <highlight>306<end> in the implant's ability and <highlight>720<end> treatment.
